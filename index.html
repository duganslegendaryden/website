About Dugan's Legendary Den

Dugan's Legendary Den is a US-based Pokémon TCG business based in Austin, TX. We're primarily operating at card shows in the Austin and the Dallas/Fort Worth areas, with plans to expand to other regions.

Our setup is built around accessibility, variety, and real-time market movement, allowing us to serve both collectors and competitive buyers with strong inventory turnover and consistent demand at live events.
